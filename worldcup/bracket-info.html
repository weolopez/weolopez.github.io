How it works

1

Pick winners & call scores

When the group stage wraps (June 27), the 32-team knockout field is set. For every match, pick who advances and predict the final score — all the way to lifting the trophy.

2

Lock it in

Brackets lock at the first knockout kickoff on June 28. No edits, no excuses. Tiebreaker: total goals in the Final.

3

Survive the madness

Earn points every round your picks survive — and stack bonus points every time you nail a scoreline. Picks cascade: if your champion goes out early, that branch of your bracket goes down with them. 💀
