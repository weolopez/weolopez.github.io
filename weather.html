Saturday, April 26: Explore & Music

  • Morning: Sightseeing
    • Visit Jackson Square & St. Louis Cathedral (0.7 miles, 15-min walk). Free to explore.
    • Grab beignets at Café du Monde (800 Decatur St., 0.8 miles, ~$3 for 3).
  • Afternoon: Garden District
    • Take St. Charles Streetcar (~$1.25 one-way, 20 min) to Garden District.
    • Explore historic mansions; lunch at Commander’s Palace (1403 Washington Ave., reservations needed, commanderspalace.com). Try Gumbo ($10).
  • Evening: Music & Dinner
    • Music: Tipitina’s (501 Napoleon Ave., rideshare ~$10). Funk and brass, shows ~9 PM, tickets ~$20 (tipitinas.com).
    • Dinner: Dooky Chase’s (2301 Orleans Ave., rideshare ~$8). Creole soul food; try Fried Chicken ($20). Reservations recommended (dookychaserestaurant.com).
