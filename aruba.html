How are you feeling today?

Morning: Essentials & Snorkel Prep

10:00 AM: Drive to Super Food Plaza to stock up on groceries, drinks, and snacks for your studio.

11:30 AM: Head back for a simple lunch and get your snorkel gear ready.

Afternoon: Local Snorkeling

1:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Stay local! Walk right over to Boca Catalina or Tres Trapi. The water is calm and clear, perfect for hours of easy snorkeling right off the beach. No driving needed!

Evening: Casual Beach Dinner

5:30 PM: Enjoy a beautiful sunset right from the beach near your studio.

7:30 PM: Walk down to The Beach Bar for a super casual, toes-in-the-sand dinner.

The Beach Bar Notes: Molly's Veggie Option: They have great veggie pizzas and salads.
Reservations: Not needed.

Morning: Lighthouse Views

11:30 AM: After grocery shopping, drive north to the California Lighthouse. Climb to the top for incredible 360-degree views of the island.

Afternoon: Arashi Beach

1:00 PM: Have lunch at Faro Blanco Restaurant (at the lighthouse) or grab a casual bite from the beach shack at nearby Arashi Beach.

2:30 PM - 4:30 PM: Relax, swim, and soak up the sun at Arashi Beach, a beautiful local favorite.

Evening: Dinner in Oranjestad

7:00 PM: Drive to The West Deck for dinner. It's a fun spot on a wooden deck over the water, perfect for sharing plates and cocktails.

The West Deck Notes: Molly's Veggie Option: Good selection of vegetarian appetizers like fried funchi, patatas bravas, and salads.
Reservations: Recommended, especially for a waterside table.

12:00 PM: Have a relaxed lunch at a casual spot in Palm Beach, like Scott's Brats or Dushi Bagels, before your dive.

Evening: Historic & Romantic Dinner

7:30 PM: After cleaning up, head to Papiamento Restaurant. Dine in a lush garden surrounding the pool of a 126-year-old manor. A true Aruban classic.

Papiamento Notes: Molly's Veggie Option: Excellent dedicated vegetarian options on their menu.

Morning: Bird Sanctuary & Lunch

9:00 AM: Visit the Bubali Bird Sanctuary, a nearby wetland reserve. Climb the observation tower for a great view of the island's birdlife.

11:30 AM: Grab an early, quick lunch on Palm Beach to fuel up for the dive.

Afternoon: Antilla Shipwreck Dive! üê†

1:00 PM: Check in at the Red Sail Pier (located behind the Hyatt Regency Hotel) for your dive.

1:30 PM - 4:30 PM: Your dive adventure to the Antilla Shipwreck. Have an amazing time exploring this piece of history!

Evening: Fresh Catch Dinner on the Water

7:30 PM: You'll be hungry post-dive. Drive south to Zeerover in Savaneta. It's a famous, ultra-casual spot where you eat the fresh catch of the day on a dock over the water.

Zeerover Notes: Molly's Veggie Option: Very limited. This is a fresh fish spot. She will likely be limited to sides like french fries, plantains, and pickled onions. Plan accordingly!
Reservations: Not taken. Just show up.

Tuesday: Explore or Unwind?

Morning & Afternoon: Arikok National Park

9:30 AM - 2:00 PM: Drive to Arikok National Park. With your rental car, you can easily visit the Fontein & Quadirikiri Caves and see the dramatic coastline at Boca Prins.

2:00 PM: Have lunch with a rugged view at the restaurant at Boca Prins.

3:30 PM Onwards: Head back to your studio to relax and escape the afternoon sun.

Evening: Woodfired Grill Dinner

7:30 PM: Head to Yemanja Woodfired Grill in Oranjestad for what many consider one of the best meals on the island.

Yemanja Notes: Molly's Veggie Option: Fantastic. They have a creative and extensive vegetarian menu section.
Reservations: Essential.

Morning: Arikok Caves

9:30 AM: Drive to Arikok National Park to explore the incredible cave systems.

Afternoon: Mangroves & Hidden Gems

12:30 PM: Head to Mangel Halto, a stunningly beautiful spot with mangroves, calm water, and great snorkeling.

2:00 PM: Enjoy a romantic, over-the-water lunch at Marina Pirata in Savaneta.

3:30 PM - 5:00 PM: Find a quiet spot at Rodger's Beach, a calmer alternative right next to Baby Beach.

Evening: Toes-in-the-Water Dinner

7:30 PM: Experience the famous Flying Fishbone. You can literally dine with your feet in the water as the sun sets.

Flying Fishbone / Marina Pirata Notes: Molly's Veggie Option: Both are heavily seafood-focused but will accommodate. Be sure to mention you need a vegetarian meal when booking. Options will be simple but available.
Reservations: ESSENTIAL for Flying Fishbone, book weeks/months ahead for the best tables. Recommended for Marina Pirata.
