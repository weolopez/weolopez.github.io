Camera Mouse Controller

Control your mouse cursor using hand gestures captured by your camera. This demo showcases real-time hand tracking with MediaPipe and virtual mouse control.

Test the Camera Mouse

How to use:

  1. Click "Start Tracking" to begin camera and hand detection
  2. Hold your hand in front of the camera with your index finger extended
  3. Move your index finger to control the mouse cursor
  4. Pinch your index finger and thumb together to click
  5. Use "Calibrate" to set up custom tracking boundaries
  6. Adjust sensitivity and smoothing for optimal control
