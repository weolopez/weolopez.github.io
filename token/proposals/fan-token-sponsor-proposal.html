The Token Program

How the Fan Token Economy Works

Fan Tokens are a community loyalty currency — earned through engagement, sponsored by local businesses, and redeemable for real-world value. Think airline miles, but hyper-local and built around Atlanta soccer.

Three actors, one ecosystem
⚽
Fans
Make picks, earn tokens, win prize pools, redeem at local sponsors
🏟️
Hosts / Organizers
Run watch parties, create prize pools to attract a crowd
🍺
Sponsors
Fund token pools in exchange for foot traffic and coupon redemptions
→ Fans earn tokens for correct picks & watch party RSVPs
→ Sponsors fund prize pools & offer token-redeemable deals
→ Hosts put up tokens to attract fans to their watch party
→ Fans spend tokens at sponsor venues — driving real foot traffic
1

A sponsor purchases a token package from Atlanta Soccer News

Brewhouse Cafe pays $50 for 5,000 tokens. No complex blockchain required — this is handled through a simple admin dashboard. They get a sponsor profile, logo placement, and a token balance.

2

The sponsor creates offers and prize pools

Brewhouse sets up two things: (a) an offer — "100 tokens = $5 off your tab," and (b) a prize pool — "500 tokens to whoever nails the USA vs Mexico score at our watch party."

3

Fans engage, earn tokens, and compete

Every correct prediction earns tokens. Fans see the Brewhouse prize pool on the USA vs Mexico match card. They're motivated to predict AND to show up at Brewhouse to be eligible.

4

The platform auto-awards the prize at full time

When the match ends and scores are set, the platform automatically finds the best predictor among Brewhouse RSVPs and transfers 500 tokens to their wallet. Push notification sent instantly.

5

Fans redeem tokens for real value

The winner (and other token-holders) can redeem 100 tokens for a $5 Brewhouse discount. They show a QR code on their phone. Brewhouse gets trackable, attributable foot traffic.

Token Economics

What Is a Token Worth?

A token is worth what the best sponsor makes it worth. Sponsors set their own redemption rates. The platform manages supply to prevent inflation and keeps the economy healthy.
